ODIN Receipe

My variation of an old Southern Living recipe
a double recipe will fill a Dutch oven
and make plenty for an extended family dinner.

Ingredients

  • Pound lean ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can petite diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 7 ounces whole wheat spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir gorund beef,onion, and garlic until beef is browned and crumblu and onion is transluncetnt, 5 to 7 minutes
  3. drain and discard excess grease
  4. stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, water , choli powder, oregano, and black pepper.
  5. bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat, and let simmer, stirring often, for about 30 minutes
  6. break spaghetti into thirds and add to the pot
  7. cover and simmer, stirring often, until pasta is tender, about 20 minutes. Serve with parmesan cheese
