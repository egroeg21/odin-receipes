ODIN Receipe

Quick, healthy, and tasty raw corn salad for the summer
no cooking required
uses fresh,raw,sweet corn, avocado and it's easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 ears corn, kernels cut from the cod
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, halved lengthwise and sliced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 small sweet onion,chopped
  • 1/4 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped or to taste
  • 1 green onion, green parts onlu, thinly sliced
  • 1 drizzle extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lime
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. mix corn kernels, tomatoes, cucumbers,avocado,onion together in a large bowl
  2. add a drizzle of olive oil.
  3. squeeze lime juice into the bowl and season with salt and pepper

Cook's note:

You can add some heat with jalapeno, or sweetness with blueberries
